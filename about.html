Our Story

  • TRIO Events is a Mumbai-based exhibition company specializing in custom booth design and global exhibitions.

  • About Trio Exhibition: Global Leaders in Brand Environments
    • The Inspiration: The Power of Three The name Trio Exhibition is inspired by the divine energy of Lord Shiva, symbolizing the harmonious execution of three core brand pillars: Events, Exhibitions, and Promotions. We believe that a brand’s success depends on the parallel growth of these interrelated functions. By mastering the synergy between creative design and technical execution, we ensure that your brand doesn't just occupy space—it commands it. ’s Premier One-Stop Exhibition Stand Contractor Trio Exhibition is recognized as a leading full-service exhibition design company and turnkey stand contractor in India. We specialize in delivering world-class, custom-built exhibition stands and high-impact retail environment displays. Beyond fabrication, Trio Exhibition is renowned for Functional Space Planning. We bridge the gap between aesthetic brilliance and strategic floor management, ensuring every square meter of your trade show booth is optimized for lead generation and visitor flow.
    Expertise That Goes "The Extra Mile" Operating in today’s competitive global market requires more than just construction; it requires a strategic partner. Trio is powered by a highly skilled team with nearly two decades of industry experience.
  • Our deep-rooted knowledge allows us to deliver: Customized Wooden Pavilions: Bespoke designs that reflect your brand’s unique identity. Technical Precision: Experts in Maxima, Octanorm, and advanced modular structures. In-House Production: A massive 9,000 sq. ft. fabrication facility that ensures quality control and cost-efficiency. Cost-Effective Solutions: Delivering designs that exceed expectations without exceeding budgets.
  • A Local Footprint with a Global Reach Headquartered with a strong presence in every major Indian metro—including Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore—we provide seamless execution across the entire country. However, our vision is global. Backed by a proven track record in the international arena, Trio Exhibition has established an extensive network of Associate Partners across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the UK. This global channel allows us to offer international exhibitors a "home-ground advantage," providing local-rate execution with world-class design standards.
